# Introduction

## 1. Background
Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) act as primary channels for companies to raise capital rapidly while simultaneously presenting retail and institutional investors with lucrative wealth-building opportunities. However, navigating the IPO landscape is inherently risky. Evaluating an IPO's genuine value requires dissecting hundreds of pages of complex Red Herring Prospectus (RHP) documents, interpreting dynamic pricing ratios, and forecasting unpredictable buyer momentum.

## 2. Motivation
Historically, retail investors have relied heavily on unstructured speculation, fragmented financial news, or manual spreadsheets. Traditional analytics platforms often supply isolated metrics without synthesis, whereas modern Generative AI models (like standard ChatGPT configurations) rely on language correlation matrices, frequently hallucinating precise numerical valuations or making up financial trends entirely. There is a critical necessity for a localized platform that combines verifiable structural mathematics with the ease of AI-driven text aggregation.

## 3. Problem Definition
It is excessively time-consuming to manually determine the impact weight of key influencing factors such as oversubscription levels across varying market participant tiers (QIB, NII, Retail), inherent company valuation, and long-term financial health (ROE, PAT). Existing unified platforms either rely strictly on fundamental ratios without predictive analytics or lean heavily on unstable models that collapse when generalizing. The challenge is constructing an analytics engine capable of deploying predictive mathematics devoid of hallucinatory capabilities, accompanied seamlessly by an unblocked, high-density visualization frontend.

## 4. Objectives of StockTraQ
StockTraQ addresses these challenges by introducing a data-first intelligence architecture. The core objectives include:
- **Numerical Regression Validation:** Deploying local, pre-trained ensemble ML models to project listing gains securely and predictably.
- **RHP Automated Extraction:** Creating an active, parameter-driven RAG chatbot to parse heavy Prospectus PDFs into readable qualitative summaries natively.
- **Microservices Deployment:** Ensuring that dense scientific calculations are structurally isolated from dashboard management CRUD streams by separating Python and Node infrastructures.
- **Visual Intelligence:** Providing an interactive verification suite allowing continuous exploration of historical backtests and comparative metrics through a premium, latency-free graphical interface.
